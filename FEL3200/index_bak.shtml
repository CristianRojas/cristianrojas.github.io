FEL 3200 System Identification

8 & 12 credits
 

News: For the Exercise Set 7, have a look at T. Söderströms notes on the computation of covariance functions (see Additional Reading).

 

Course objectives

The aim of this course is to provide the fundamentals of data based modeling. It is possible to choose between an 8 credits version of the course aimed at PhD students who are interested in a general understanding of the problem and how to use system identification, and a 12 credits version aimed at students specializing in related areas, such as control theory and signal processing. The latter version provide more in depth-coverage of the theoretical basis.  Basic paradigms are discussed and state of  the art methods are covered in depth.
