The Segway challenge consists of building a Segway-like vehicle in LEGO Mindstorms, and designing a control system which gives it superior performance to that of your competitors. We are going to run two competitions:

  1. Marathon balancing: try to make your Segway balance for as long time as possible

  1. The 10 meter dash: have your Segway drive 10 meter in a better time than your competitors.
